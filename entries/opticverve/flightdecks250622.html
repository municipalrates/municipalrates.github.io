Ever decoding the shapes we leave on the ground. Looking down on Kansas, I think of pressing each of these fields like buttons.

They could easily change colour, or start to spin. Each one the digital representation of a record playing. An impossible feat to mix them all into one co-ordinated sound.

The space we've left to inhabit beneath our wings could be immense. We irrigate in circles, endless rotations, even as we run ourselves out.
